Acustica has been trained exclusively on material authored and developed by Giuliano Nicoletti:
– the book *Mastering the Sound of the Acoustic Guitar*  
– the *Mastering Training* course  
– research papers and measurement methods  
– analytical tools and tonewood databases from the Iulius Guitars website.

It does not use or reproduce any copyrighted material from other authors.

Acustica’s predictive models, measurement routines, optimisation processes, and tonewood data are original works by Giuliano Nicoletti.  
They are protected intellectual property.

Acustica also acknowledges the foundational academic research that established the scientific framework of guitar acoustics — notably the contributions of Trevor Gore, Ian M. Firth, Erik Jansson, Bernard Richardson, Graham Caldersmith, and others.

The information provided by Acustica is for study, research, and design support.  
It should not be interpreted as prescriptive instruction, but as an analytical companion to the builder’s own experience.
